By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. --- # 1. Overview BMI Calculator is a utility application designed to calculate Body Mass Index (BMI) based on information entered by the user. Most processing in the App occurs locally on the user’s device. However, the App may use third-party services for advertising, diagnostics, and crash reporting. --- # 2. Health Data The App allows users to manually enter the following information: * Height * Weight * Age (optional) * Gender (optional) This information may be considered **health-related data**. Important information about this data: * It is used **only to calculate BMI** * It is processed **locally on the user's device** * The developer **does not collect, store, or transmit this health data to external servers** The App does not create health profiles or provide medical recommendations. --- # 3.
